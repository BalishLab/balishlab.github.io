Welcome to Our Lab

We investigate the fundamental biology of mycoplasmas—unusual bacteria with minimal genomes that cause chronic human infections. Our research aims to develop new therapeutic strategies to combat these important pathogens.

Our Research Focus

🔬 Structural Biology

Investigating attachment organelles and novel cytoskeletal proteins unique to mycoplasmas

🦠 Biofilm Formation

Understanding how biofilms regulate virulence factors in chronic infections

🧬 Host Interactions

Characterizing pathogen-host cell interactions from adhesion to invasion

🔄 Cell Division

Exploring division mechanisms in wall-less bacteria
