Research

Our laboratory focuses on understanding the biology and pathogenesis of Mycoplasma species. We investigate host-pathogen interactions, molecular mechanisms of infection, and develop novel therapeutic approaches.

Current Projects

  • Mycoplasma motility and gliding mechanisms
  • Host immune response to mycoplasma infections
  • Novel antimicrobial strategies
