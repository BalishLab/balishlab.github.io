🎓 Join Our Lab

We welcome motivated graduate and undergraduate students interested in microbiology research. Our lab provides hands-on experience with cutting-edge techniques in molecular biology, cell biology, and biochemistry.

What we offer:

  • Training in modern molecular and cellular biology techniques
  • Mentorship from experienced researchers
  • Opportunities to contribute to important research on human pathogens
  • A collaborative and supportive lab environment
  • Potential for publication and conference presentations

Interested in joining our lab? Contact Dr. Balish at balishmf@miamioh.edu to discuss available opportunities.
